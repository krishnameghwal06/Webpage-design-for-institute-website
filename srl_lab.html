Sensor Research Lab

Sensor Research Lab in Electronics Engineering Department contains the well-established research facilities in the field of microwave and optical. It is equipped with all the latest and high end instruments like Fabrication of Prototype Notch Filter (W-band)(75-105GHz), Surface Plasmon Resonance System, Omricon lab model-Bode 100, Laser Spectrum Analyzer, Field Optical Spectrum Analyzer, Programmable Fusion Splicer Machine, High resolution Spectrometer and Programmable dc power supply to do the research in Microwave and optical field.
